Security First

Your Data,
Immutably Protected

Every document you upload is encrypted, blockchain-notarized, and protected by enterprise-grade security measures. Compliant with GDPR and RBI standards.

AES-256 Encryption

Military-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Your financial documents are unreadable to unauthorized parties.

Blockchain Document Vault

Documents are hashed and recorded on an immutable blockchain ledger via IPFS, creating tamper-proof proof of authenticity.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Biometric and OTP-based MFA is triggered automatically on suspicious login patterns or high-risk transactions.

Real-Time Anomaly Detection

The Security Agent monitors your access patterns 24/7 and flags anomalies before they become breaches.
