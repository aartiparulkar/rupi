Category:
  - Growth
Pre Advice:
  - Focus on growth assets like equity mutual funds and index funds, Use SIP for regular investing, Avoid market timing
Equity Investments:
  Description:
    - Equity investments focus on growth by investing in stock markets through mutual funds or index funds.
  Equity Mutual Funds:
    Safe Risk:
      Returns:
        - ~10% – 12%
      Which One:
        - Large Cap Funds
      Examples:
        - SBI Bluechip Fund
        - ICICI Prudential Bluechip Fund
      Best For:
        - Stable growth with lower risk
    Medium Risk:
      Returns:
        - ~12% – 15%
      Which One:
        - Flexi Cap Funds
      Examples:
        - Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund
        - UTI Flexi Cap Fund
      Best For:
        - Balanced growth across market segments
    High Risky:
      Returns:
        - ~15% – 18%+
      Which One:
        - Mid Cap Funds
      Examples:
        - Kotak Emerging Equity Fund
        - Axis Midcap Fund
      Best For:
        - Higher returns with higher volatility
      Advice:
        - Limit allocation to 20-30% of portfolio
  Index Funds:
    Description:
      - Index funds track market indices and provide stable long-term returns with low cost.
    Safe Risk:
      Returns:
        - ~10% – 12%
      Which One:
        - Nifty 50 Index Funds, Sensex Index Funds
      Examples:
        - UTI Nifty 50 Index Fund
        - HDFC Index Fund Sensex Plan
      Best For:
        - Beginners and passive investors
    Benefits:
      - Low expense ratio
      - No fund manager risk
      - Consistent market returns
  Sip Strategy:
    Description:
      - Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly.
    Rules:
      - Invest monthly instead of lump sum
      - Continue SIP during market ups and downs
      - Increase SIP amount with income growth
    Example:
      - Invest ₹3000 per month in a Nifty 50 index fund for 5+ years
    Benefits:
      - Reduces market timing risk
      - Builds disciplined investing habit
      - Averages out market volatility
Post Advice:
  - Invest for long term (minimum 5+ years) to reduce risk
  - Do not panic during market fluctuations
  - Avoid investing lump sum in volatile markets, prefer SIP
  - Diversify across large-cap, flexi-cap and mid-cap funds
  - Do not chase past high-performing funds
  - Increase investment gradually as income grows
  - Review portfolio every 6–12 months
  - Limit mid-cap exposure to avoid high volatility
  - Stay consistent with SIP even during market downturns
  - Focus on disciplined investing rather than short-term gains
